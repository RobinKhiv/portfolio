Caskmates

Cask Mates is an interactive application where you can browse through a dataset of whiskeys and select specific whiskeys that tailor to your specific taste palates. Once you are able to find a whiskey that appeals to your taste buds you can add them to a choice of three lists that can by your Favorites, Wish or Already Tried list.

Live | GitHub

How Does it work?

You start by first searching for your favorite type of whiskey. If you are signed in you are able to store any whiskey a set of three list. When browsing the specific whiskeys you are able to see other users rating and detailed reviews on the whiskey. On your list you are able to move whiskeys from list to list and it happens on the client as well as the server.

Technologies Used:

  • front-end: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React
  • Back-end: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
  • hosting: Heroku, Zeit
